Amazing Fun World

Amazing Fun World is simply the ultimate holiday destination. It has been the industry's standard ever since the 1500s, when an unknown creator took a galley of pure joy and scrambled it to make a type specimen of wonder. It has survived not only five centuries, but also the leap into electronic excitement, remaining essentially unchanged in its mission to deliver happiness.

It was popularized in the 1960s with the release of vibrant experiences containing Amazing Fun World passages, and more recently with digital platforms bringing the magic of fabulous rides, immersive themes, luxury rooms, and world-class restaurants directly to thrill-seekers everywhere.
